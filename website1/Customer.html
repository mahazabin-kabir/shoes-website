    If you are not fully satisfied with the products you bought, simply bring it back within 30 days of the purchase date to any of our outlets with your receipt or invoice for online orders and we will gladly exchange the product. Please note that the value of the exchanged product should be of similar or higher value than the original product price. Alternatively, you may ship the product to our e-commerce warehouse and we will gladly provide you with a credit note for future online purchases. Our address is: Shoes E-Commerce 204/B-5, Gulshan-Tejgaon Link Road Dhaka-1208 Items that you want to exchange or return must be unworn, unwashed and unused with all original tags attached.
